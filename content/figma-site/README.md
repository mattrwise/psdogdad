## Structure notes from the Figma site

- Resources hub (/resources) is 4 cards: Health & Wellness, Training Resources,
  Dog Dad Handbook, Recommended Products.
- Health & Wellness is also a top-level nav item.
- The vet directory has city filters (12 Coachella Valley cities) and specialty
  filters (Specialty Care, General Practice, Urgent Care, Mobile Service, Emergency),
  plus an "Add a Veterinarian" button.
- Training modules offer "Download Module PDF"; handbook offers PDF downloads
  (Complete Handbook, New Dog Dad Checklist, Health Record).
- Product cards show brand, star rating, review count, "why we love it", price
  range — all sample/illustrative data, explicitly not affiliated with brands.
