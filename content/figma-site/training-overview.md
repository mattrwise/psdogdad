## Core Training Principles

- 🎯 **Consistency is Key** — Use the same commands and rules every time. Everyone in your household should be on the same page.
- 🎉 **Positive Reinforcement** — Reward desired behaviors immediately. Treats, praise, and play are powerful motivators.
- ⏱️ **Patience & Timing** — Training takes time. Mark good behavior instantly and keep sessions short (5-15 minutes).
- 🎮 **Make it Fun** — Training should be enjoyable for both of you. End on a positive note and keep energy high.

## Common Training Mistakes (And How to Fix Them)

- ✗ **Inconsistent Commands** → ✓ Solution: Choose one word per command and stick with it. 'Down' shouldn't mean both 'lie down' and 'get off the couch.'
- ✗ **Training When Frustrated** → ✓ Solution: If you're frustrated, take a break. Dogs pick up on your emotions and training becomes counterproductive.
- ✗ **Too Much Too Soon** → ✓ Solution: Master one command before moving to the next. Build a strong foundation before advancing.
- ✗ **Punishment-Based Methods** → ✓ Solution: Focus on rewarding good behavior rather than punishing bad. Positive methods build trust and confidence.
- ✗ **Expecting Perfection** → ✓ Solution: Every dog learns at their own pace. Celebrate small wins and progress over perfection.
