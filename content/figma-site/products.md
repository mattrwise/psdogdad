## Smart Shopping Tips

- 💰 **Quality Over Quantity** — Invest in durable, well-made products. They cost more upfront but save money long-term.
- 🔍 **Read Reviews** — Check what other dog dads say. Real experiences are more valuable than marketing.
- 📏 **Size Matters** — Always check sizing charts. A harness or bed that doesn't fit is money wasted.
- 🐕 **Know Your Dog** — Consider your dog's personality, size, and needs. What works for others might not work for you.
- 🌱 **Eco-Friendly Options** — Look for sustainable materials and brands that give back to animal causes.
- ⚠️ **Safety First** — Check for recalls and safety certifications. Your dog's health is worth the research.
